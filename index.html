Here is a list of major Ikaria Juice Vitamins & Ingredients along with their benefits: 

1

Fucoxanthin

Fucoxanthin is a xanthophyll, a type of carotenoid found in brown algae. It has various therapeutic uses and is widely used to treat obesity by lowering ceramide levels. It also has potential benefits in addressing tumors, diabetes, and cardiovascular issues.
2

Capsaicin

Capsaicin is a compound found in chili peppers that is known for its heat-producing qualities. In the context of Ikaria Juice, capsaicin is included to boost the body’s natural metabolism, aiding in the process of weight loss.
3

Dandelion

Dandelion is a common herb that has been used in traditional medicine for centuries. It is known to promote digestion and increase appetite. Some studies have found that dandelion root extract can help in weight loss and reducing cholesterol levels.
4

Silymarin

Silymarin is an active ingredient derived from the milk thistle plant. It's an anti-inflammatory and antioxidant treatment that's beneficial to the ceramide, a type of lipid molecule that plays a role in cellular signaling.
5

Panax Ginseng

Panax Ginseng, also known as Asian Ginseng, is a herbal root widely used in various Asian herbal preparations. It is believed to help the body burn fat and gain energy, and it might also boost physical and sexual performance.
6

Resveratrol

Resveratrol is a natural antioxidant found in grapes, wine, and certain other fruits. It is linked to weight loss, longevity, healthy inflammation, and a range of other effects. In Ikaria Juice, it is included for its potential benefits in obesity, cardiovascular health, diabetes, and other conditions.
7

Citrus Pectin

Citrus Pectin is a natural source of fiber. Like other types of fiber, citrus pectin can enhance feelings of fullness, suppressing your appetite. It soaks up water in your stomach and digestive tract, tricking your body into thinking you’ve eaten more food.
8

EGCG

EGCG, or Epigallocatechin gallate, is a type of catechin found in green tea. It is known for its antioxidant properties and is believed to have numerous health benefits, including potential effects on weight loss and cardiovascular health.
